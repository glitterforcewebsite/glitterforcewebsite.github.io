Glitter Force
Glitter Force is about Jubiland the kingdom of happy endings that has been invaded by the Shadow Realm. Queen Euphoria sends Candy to Earth to find the Glitter Force. The members are Glitter Lucky (Emily), Glitter Sunny (Kelsey), Glitter Peace (Lily), Glitter Spring (April), and Glitter Breeze (Chloe).


Glitter Force Doki Doki
Glitter Force Doki Doki is about Splendorius that has been invaded by the Mercenares. Princess Marie Angelic sends Glitter Spade to Earth to find the rest of the Glitter Force. The members are Glitter Hear (Maya), Glitter Diamond (Rachel), Glitter Clover (Clara), Glitter Spade (Makenzie), and Glitter Ace (Nataile).


Glitter Force Shiny Rainbow
Glitter Force Shiny Rainbow is the sequel to Glitter Force about Jubiland the kingdom of happy endings that has been invaded by the Shadow Force. Queen Candy sends Glitter Rose to Earth to find the Glitter Force. The members are Glitter Ruby (Jessica), Glitter Rose (Scarlet), Glitter Aurelia (Amber), Glitter Daisy (Christa), Glitter Emerald (Jade), Glitter Aqua (Azora), Glitter Violet (Lydia), and Glitter Elva (Kenzie).


Glitter Force Bright Shadows
Glitter Force Bright Shadow is the sequel to Glitter Force Shiny Rainbow about Jubiland the kingdom of happy endings that has been invaded by the Shadow Force. Queen Candy goes to Earth to find the new Glitter Force. They are outcasts, but they willing to fight for the good of everyone. The members are Glitter Night (Luna) and Glitter Day (Chloe).
