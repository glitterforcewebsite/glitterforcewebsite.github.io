On This Page You Can Team Up With Friends and make your very own Glitter Force!

There are two types of Glitter Forces; a team force and a partner force. In a team force you have 3-8 Glitter Warriors who can all transform seperately. In a partner force you have 2 Glitter Warriors who have to be together to transform.

Pros of a Team Force

  • - all members can transform seperately

  • - all members can attack seperately

  • - 4-8 members can be on the team

Pros of a Partner Force

  • - good for people who are less social

  • - members are stronger together

  • - less members are required for a team
